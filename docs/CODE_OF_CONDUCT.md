# DevConnect Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders of the **DevConnect** community pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Community leaders (project maintainers) are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, or other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct. Reasons for moderation decisions will be communicated when appropriate.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all DevConnect community spaces, including:

- GitHub repositories, issues, pull requests, and discussions  
- Social media channels related to DevConnect  
- Community events (virtual or in-person)  
- Any official DevConnect communication channels  

It also applies when representing DevConnect in public spaces.

## Enforcement Process

### 1. Correction
**Impact:** Minor inappropriate or unprofessional behavior  

**Consequence:** Private written warning with explanation and guidance. A public apology may be requested.

---

### 2. Warning
**Impact:** Repeated or moderate violations  

**Consequence:** Formal warning with restrictions on interaction for a defined period.

---

### 3. Temporary Ban
**Impact:** Serious or sustained violations  

**Consequence:** Temporary ban from community participation for a specified duration.

---

### 4. Permanent Ban
**Impact:** Severe or repeated violations, harassment, or abuse  

**Consequence:** Permanent removal from all DevConnect community spaces.
